Plot:
There are 25 humans, and 1 of them is a killer. The killer will murder someone every night, until there are just a few humans and the village is destroyed. You are the Sheriff and you have to find the killer before he gets to you!

Gameplay:
Press "Continue" to go through one night.
After every night you will get up to 3 new messages, bit be careful, not all those messages are true.
Every human can be inprisoned, meaning that he can not kill or be killed at night. You can inprison any human by pressing the prison icon at the top-right of every human's square
Double click to kill any suspicious human.

Warning: Contains Audio
Please keep in mind that this is just a game.
